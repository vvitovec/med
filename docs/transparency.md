# Transparency Statement

Trust Coupons is designed to be better aligned than traditional coupon extensions.

## Ranking

Coupons are ranked by:

1. verified lowest final checkout total
2. observed savings
3. recent success rate
4. freshness
5. source confidence

Affiliate commission is not included in the ranking model.

## Affiliate Disclosure

The product may earn affiliate revenue when a user chooses to use supported partner links or coupons. This must be visible before affiliate-related actions. Affiliate revenue must not change which coupon is shown as best.

## Privacy

Default telemetry is minimal and anonymous. Local-only mode sends no coupon-attempt telemetry.

The product must not collect:

- personal profiles
- names, emails, addresses, or payment details
- full cart contents
- unrelated browsing history
- data for resale

## Coupon Sources

Coupon records should preserve source metadata. Unverified community or aggregator codes stay pending until verified or reviewed.
